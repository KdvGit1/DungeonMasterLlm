# Dice Roll Required — Action Examples

## ALWAYS require a dice roll (uncertain outcome):

### Charisma Checks
- "I try to persuade", "I convince", "I talk him into", "I negotiate" → Charisma DC 10-15
- "I lie", "I bluff", "I pretend", "I deceive" → Charisma DC 12-16
- "I intimidate", "I threaten", "I scare" → Charisma DC 12-15
- "I seduce", "I charm", "I flatter" → Charisma DC 11-14

### Wisdom Checks
- "I try to sense if lying", "I read his intentions", "I check for hidden motives" → Wisdom (Insight) DC 13
- "I listen carefully", "I eavesdrop", "I try to hear" → Wisdom (Perception) DC 11-14
- "I watch for movement", "I look for danger", "I keep watch" → Wisdom (Perception) DC 12

### Intelligence Checks
- "I search the room", "I examine", "I investigate", "I look for clues" → Intelligence DC 12-15
- "I try to remember", "I recall", "I identify" → Intelligence DC 10-14
- "I figure out the mechanism", "I solve the puzzle" → Intelligence DC 13-17

### Dexterity Checks
- "I sneak", "I hide", "I move silently", "I follow without being seen" → Dexterity (Stealth) DC 13-16
- "I pick the lock", "I pickpocket", "I disarm the trap" → Dexterity DC 14-18
- "I dodge", "I jump out of the way" → Dexterity DC 12-15

### Strength Checks
- "I break down the door", "I force it open", "I lift" → Strength DC 13-18
- "I climb", "I pull myself up" → Strength (Athletics) DC 12-16
- "I grapple", "I wrestle", "I hold him down" → Strength DC 13

### Attack Rolls
- "I attack", "I strike", "I stab", "I shoot", "I hit" → d20 + Strength (melee) or Dexterity (ranged) vs enemy AC

## NEVER require a dice roll (certain outcome):

- "I talk to", "I greet", "I say hello" → no roll
- "I ask about", "I question him" → no roll (unless trying to extract secrets)
- "I walk to", "I go to", "I enter", "I approach" → no roll
- "I look around" (casual) → no roll
- "I sit down", "I eat", "I rest" → no roll
- "I buy something" (with enough gold) → no roll
- "I read a sign", "I open an unlocked door" → no roll
